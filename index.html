About Me

I am a local maths teacher, providing private tuition in the Wigan, Warrington and St. Helens area.

I have ten years experience teaching mathematics across the entire spectrum of secondary school education covering all topics of multiple exam board syllabi. I can help to build the confidence of your child by analysing the areas in where there can be improvement and bridging those vital knowledge gaps. I can help your child to achieve their full potential in mathematics to ensure they get the grades that they deserve and an understanding of the topics that will last them into adulthood.

My prices start at a basic rate of £30 per hour for a private one-to-one session, usually arranged weekly after school or over the weekend. Please get in touch to check my availability and to arrange a first session.
